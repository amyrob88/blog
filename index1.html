About me

I am currently a stay at home mum, who is looking at going back to work, and creating a career for myself. After having 9 years out of work, and only having experience in customer services, I decided I wanted a change. And that my career should be in IT, as it is a growing industry, but with a lack of certified and experienced people for those rolls that are available. In my spare time, I play video games, read around my chosen career choice, practice building websites, and I also have an allotment, which I go to most weekends with my children and my partner. My partner has helped me when it comes to coding and learning about the IT industry, as he is a back end developer, who started as a front end developer. He not only has 10 years experience in the IT industry but he also has certifications.

Why I chose codenation.

I had an advert pop up on Facebook for the cyber security course, and as someone who has been looking at going into the IT sector, especially after 9 years of being unemployed, due to having children, i thought this would be a great opportunity, not only to gain qualifications, but to have the potential to get an apprenticeship, learning and gaining experience in field i very much want to be a part of. i believe what code nation are doing is amazing. I also, very much, would like to be apart of bridging the gap of women in this area.

Why technology and coding

Technology is becoming more and more available to everybody, of any age. and most day to day is affected by technology. I have always had an interest in technology, it started with my love for video games, playing on consoles like playstation, Nintendo, and portable consoles, not to mention PC gaming. As i am getting older, i am finding the working of software and computers far more interesting. And seeing the fished product, i appreciate the time and money that must go in to creating games and software for the masses.
